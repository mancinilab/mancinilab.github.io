Team

We are keen on supporting postdoctoral candidates interested in joining our team to funding applications such as: EMBO, HFSP, Wellcome, Marie Sklodowska-Curie, BBSRC, Daphne Jackson, Leverhulme, Newton. Whether postdoc or student, if you feel an affinity to our research questions and would like to learn more, email Leonardo directly.

What we do

We are curious about small life and its impact on human and animal health, and on the environment. We satisfy our curiosity using microscopes, and we try to break barriers between disciplines.
